# Data Source

## Company

Apple Inc.

## Data Source

Apple Annual Reports (Form 10-K)

Official financial statements released by Apple Inc.

## Analysis Period

Fiscal Year:

- 2021
- 2022
- 2023
- 2024

## Financial Statements Used

This project uses three major financial statements:

1. Income Statement
   - Revenue
   - Cost of Sales
   - Gross Profit
   - Operating Income
   - Net Income

2. Balance Sheet
   - Total Assets
   - Total Liabilities
   - Shareholders' Equity
   - Cash and Cash Equivalents

3. Cash Flow Statement
   - Operating Cash Flow
   - Capital Expenditure
   - Free Cash Flow

## Purpose

The purpose of this project is to analyze Apple's:

- Revenue growth trend
- Profitability performance
- Financial risk
- Cash flow quality

by applying financial analysis and data analytics techniques.
